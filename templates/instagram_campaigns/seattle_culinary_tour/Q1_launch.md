# Q1: Launch & Brand Awareness

**Focus**: Introduce the concept, highlight the exclusivity, and visually stun the audience.
**Goal**: Build a follower base and sell out the first 3 tours.

## Theme: "The Hidden Masters of Seattle's Kitchens"

### Month 1: The Teaser
- **Week 1**: 
  - *Post 1 (Reel)*: Fast-paced montage of kitchen fire, plating, and Seattle skyline. Text: "Seattle's culinary scene is about to change. Are you on the list?"
  - *Post 2 (Image)*: High-contrast macro shot of a complex dish. Caption: "Taste what the Executive Chefs eat. Led by the Sous-Chefs who make the magic happen."
- **Week 2**: 
  - *Post 1 (Carousel)*: "Meet Your Guide: Chef Marcus." 3 slides detailing his background at a Michelin-level spot.
  - *Post 2 (Story)*: AMA question box: "What's your dream Seattle restaurant?"
- **Week 3**:
  - *Post 1 (Reel)*: The Walkthrough. A POV shot of walking into a highly sought-after restaurant through the kitchen door.
  - *Post 2 (Image)*: Tour dates drop. "40 Seats. 4 Restaurants. 1 Unforgettable Night."
- **Week 4**:
  - *Post 1 (Carousel)*: The Menu Teaser. Blurry photos that come into focus on the last slide. CTA: Link in bio to secure tickets.
  - *Post 2 (Reel)*: Chef plating a dish with the caption: "Your $250 ticket gets you this, and more."

### Month 2 & 3: The Proof
- Focus on sharing the first tour's success.
- **UGC (User Generated Content)**: Reposting stories from the first 40 guests.
- **Highlight Reels**: "What a sold-out night looks like."
- **Urgency**: "Next tour is 80% booked. Don't miss out."
