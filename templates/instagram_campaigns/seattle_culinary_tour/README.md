# Seattle Culinary Tour - Annual Instagram Campaign Strategy

## Business Overview
- **Service**: Exclusive restaurant tours led by sous-chefs in Seattle.
- **Capacity**: 40 seats per tour.
- **Price**: $250 per ticket.
- **Experience**: Top meals prepared specifically for tour members at elite Seattle restaurants.

## Campaign Objectives
1. **Brand Awareness**: Establish the tours as the premier luxury culinary experience in Seattle.
2. **Engagement**: Build a community of food enthusiasts and high-ticket buyers.
3. **Conversion**: Sell out all 40 seats for every scheduled tour.
4. **Retention**: Encourage repeat bookings and user-generated content (UGC).

## Target Audience
- Affluent locals and tourists seeking unique dining experiences.
- Foodies, culinary enthusiasts, and corporate team-building organizers.
- Demographics: 25-55 years old, high disposable income.

## Content Pillars
1. **The Culinary Art**: High-quality visuals of the dishes.
2. **The Sous-Chefs**: Stories and behind-the-scenes with the guides.
3. **The Experience**: ambiance, tour members enjoying the tour.
4. **Exclusivity**: Scarcity-driven posts (e.g., "Only 5 seats left").

## Annual Breakdown
- **Q1: Launch & Awareness** (Establishing the brand and the offering)
- **Q2: Engagement & Trust** (Building community and showcasing the chefs)
- **Q3: Conversion & Scaling** (Maximizing ticket sales during peak seasons)
- **Q4: Retention & Holidays** (Corporate bookings, gift cards, and VIP loyalty)

## Best Practices
- **Visuals**: Professional photography, short-form Reels (15-30s), carousel posts for menus.
- **Captions**: Engaging hooks, storytelling, clear Call-to-Actions (CTAs).
- **Posting Frequency**: 4-5 times a week (Mix of Reels, Carousels, and single images).
- **Stories**: Daily updates, countdowns to tours, behind-the-scenes snippets.
