# Q4: Retention, Holidays & Corporate

**Focus**: Wrapping up the year, pushing corporate buyouts for holiday parties, and selling gift cards for the following year.
**Goal**: Secure 10 full corporate buyouts (selling all 40 seats at once) and maximize gift card revenue.

## Theme: "Give the Gift of Taste"

### Content Strategy

**1. Corporate Buyout Push (October/Early November)**
- *Reel*: Professional, sleek video showing a group having a tailored experience. The chefs addressing the private group.
- *Caption*: "Tired of the standard office holiday party? Treat your team to a 5-course journey led by Seattle's top sous-chefs. Private buyouts (40 seats) available for December. Inquire via DM."

**2. The Holiday Gift Card Campaign (Late November/December)**
- *Carousel*: Beautiful physical or digital gift cards styled next to premium ingredients or wine glasses.
- *Caption*: "The hardest reservation in Seattle makes the best gift. Secure a 2027 Tour Gift Card before we release the public schedule."

**3. The 'Year in Review' (Late December)**
- *Reel*: A montage of the best moments, dishes, and guest reactions from the entire year.
- *Caption*: "Over 2,000 plates served. Countless memories made. Thank you, Seattle, for an incredible year. Here's to what we're cooking up next."

**4. VIP Waitlist Teasers**
- For guests who attended this year, offer them early access to next year's dates.
- *Story*: "Our VIP list gets 48-hour early access to the Spring dates. Check your inbox."
- *Post (Image)*: An empty, beautifully set table for 40. "We're holding a seat for you next year. Sign up for the newsletter to know first."
