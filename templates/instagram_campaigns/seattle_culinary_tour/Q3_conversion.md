# Q3: Conversion & Scaling (Peak Season)

**Focus**: Drive ticket sales hard for the summer and early fall seasons. Capitalize on tourists and locals looking for premium weekend plans.
**Goal**: Sell out all tours 4 weeks in advance. Maintain a waitlist.

## Theme: "The Ultimate Seattle Ticket"

### Content Strategy

**1. Scarcity-Driven Posts**
- *Reel*: A fast-paced countdown of tickets selling out. Flashing numbers "40... 25... 10... 0."
- *Caption*: "Friday night is gone. Saturday only has 4 seats left. Don't say we didn't warn you. Book at the link in bio."

**2. The FOMO (Fear Of Missing Out) Stories**
- Daily stories highlighting the most spectacular moments from the previous night's tour (e.g., flambéing a dessert tableside, pouring a rare vintage wine).
- *Overlay Text*: "Wish you were here?" with a direct link sticker to the booking page.

**3. Collaborations & Influencers**
- Partner with 2-3 high-profile Seattle food or lifestyle influencers per month.
- Give them 2 complimentary tickets in exchange for a dedicated Reel and Story sequence during the tour.
- *Format*: POV "Come with me on Seattle's most exclusive culinary tour."

**4. The 'Menu Drop' Carousel**
- Announcing the seasonal menu changes for late summer/early fall.
- Extreme close-ups of the new dishes.
- *Caption*: "The Autumn Harvest Menu is here. Featuring wild-foraged truffles and King Salmon. These 40 seats won't last the week."

### Advertising Boost
- Allocate budget to boost the "Menu Drop" and Influencer Reels to targeted demographics (High-income earners, 30-55, interests in fine dining and luxury travel within a 50-mile radius of Seattle).
