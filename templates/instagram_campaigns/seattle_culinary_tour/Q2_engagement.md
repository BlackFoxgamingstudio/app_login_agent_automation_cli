# Q2: Engagement & Trust Building

**Focus**: Deepen the connection with the audience by showcasing the expertise and personality of the sous-chefs.
**Goal**: Increase organic reach, engagement rates, and establish the tours as a 'must-do' Seattle experience.

## Theme: "Behind the Line"

### Content Strategy

**1. The "How-To" Mini-Series (Reels)**
- Short, highly-produced videos of the sous-chefs teaching a simple, elegant technique (e.g., "How to perfectly sear a scallop in 60 seconds").
- *Caption*: "Bring a taste of the tour to your kitchen. To taste the real thing, join Chef [Name] this Friday. Link in bio."

**2. Supplier Spotlights (Carousel/Images)**
- Highlighting the local Pacific Northwest ingredients.
- *Slide 1*: Beautiful shot of fresh Pike Place Market seafood.
- *Slide 2*: The chef selecting the ingredients.
- *Slide 3*: The final dish served on the tour.
- *Caption*: "We don't just eat; we trace the journey. From the Puget Sound to your plate."

**3. Interactive Stories (Weekly)**
- "Guess the Ingredient" polls.
- "This or That" plating choices.
- Live Q&A with a sous-chef from the prep kitchen (15 mins).

**4. Social Proof & Testimonials**
- Beautiful portraits of guests holding a glass of wine laughing, with a quote overlay.
- *Caption*: "An anniversary they won't forget. We only have 40 seats a night to keep it intimate. Grab yours for next month."

### Posting Cadence
- **Monday**: Educational Reel (Chef tip).
- **Wednesday**: High-quality static image (Dish or ambiance).
- **Friday**: Urgency/Sales post (Carousel or Reel showing the tour vibe with a CTA for weekend bookings).
- **Saturday/Sunday**: Real-time stories from the ongoing tours.
